Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 December 2025

Overview

TPower2 respects user privacy and is committed to handling personal information in a responsible and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as username and contact details
  • Verification and security-related data where required
  • Transaction identifiers (full payment card details are not stored)
  • Usage data including IP address, browser type, and access timestamps
  • Cookies and site preferences to improve user experience

How We Use Information

Collected data is used to operate and maintain the platform, provide customer support, process transactions, enhance security, and comply with applicable legal requirements. TPower2 does not sell personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Requests can be made by contacting support@tpower2.net.

Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. While we strive to protect user data, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users are encouraged to protect their account credentials.
